Methodology & Assumptions

  • Competitor Gap Keywords: Keywords where 2+ top competitors rank positions 4-10 that you don't currently rank for
  • Commercial Keywords: Transactional/commercial intent queries (buy, best, review, vs, etc.)
  • Blended CTR: Industry-specific CTR data for positions 1-5 (use your tool's category-specific values)
  • Incremental Revenue: Projections show gains above current baseline traffic
  • Monte Carlo Forecast: Simulates 1,000+ growth trajectories over 12 months with randomized variations in ranking speed, CTR performance, and conversion rates. Shows P10 (conservative), P50 (expected), and P90 (optimistic) confidence intervals. Growth follows S-curve pattern reflecting typical SEO ramp-up.
  • Timelines: Conservative (3-6 months), Moderate (6-12 months), Optimistic (12+ months)
  • Variables: Results vary based on competition, seasonality, content quality, and technical SEO execution
